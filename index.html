What is CivicConnect

A Complete Civic Services Ecosystem

Pakistan's first unified Smart City community platform bringing together emergency services, healthcare, NGOs, government offices, and civic resources into one intelligent portal.

Purpose and Mission

To empower every citizen with instant access to vital community services, emergency support, and public welfare programs — eliminating barriers between people and the help they need.

Smart City Integration

Built on Smart City governance principles, integrating real-time data from hospitals, rescue services, and government bodies to provide accurate, up-to-date civic information.

Community Support

Connecting volunteers, blood donors, NGO workers, and community leaders to create a resilient network of civic helpers who respond to both daily needs and emergencies.

Emergency Response

One-tap SOS with live GPS location sharing, direct connection to ambulance, police, fire brigade, and rescue services — reducing critical response times significantly.

Transparent Governance

All registered services undergo admin verification before public listing. Citizens can report inaccuracies, ensuring the platform maintains high data quality and public trust.

Social Impact

By digitizing civic infrastructure, we reduce emergency response times, increase blood donation rates, and improve access to welfare programs for underserved communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my organization on CivicConnect?
Go to the Register Service tab and fill out the complete form including your organization name, category, contact details, address, and description. Upload your logo. Our admin team reviews your submission within 24 to 48 hours. Once approved, your organization appears in the services directory with a verification badge.
How does the Emergency SOS system work?
When you press the SOS button, the system immediately captures your browser's live GPS coordinates using the Geolocation API and submits an emergency request to Firestore. The request includes your name, emergency type, exact latitude and longitude, and a timestamp. Admin staff and nearby emergency units are notified in real time.
How does the live map integration work?
The Emergency tab features a live Leaflet.js map showing all registered services as color-coded markers — blue for hospitals, red for blood banks, green for NGOs. Click any marker to see the service name, phone number, and address. Location permission is requested for nearest-service detection.
How does the admin approval system work?
All service registrations start with a Pending status in Firestore. Our admin team verifies the organization's legitimacy by checking registration documents, contacting the phone number, and confirming the address. Approved services receive a blue verification badge visible to all users on the platform.
How do I find blood donors near me?
Filter the services directory by Blood Bank to see all registered blood banks with contact numbers and availability status. For urgent needs, registered donors matching your required blood type in your city receive push notifications. You can also call 1122 for emergency blood coordination.
Is CivicConnect free to use?
Yes, CivicConnect is completely free for citizens. Searching services, using emergency features, receiving announcements, and earning volunteer badges are all free. Organizations can also register their services for free. The platform is supported by government partnerships and NGO collaborations.
How do I earn volunteer badges and rewards?
Badges are earned automatically based on civic activities logged on the platform. Donate blood through a registered partner blood bank, volunteer through an NGO partner, assist in emergency response, or share community alerts to accumulate points. Points unlock badge tiers visible on your public profile.
